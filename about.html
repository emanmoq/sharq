About AlSarq Academia

Al Sharq Academia is an online platform offering an eclectic range of academic courses that aim at disseminating knowledge from the social sciences and humanities, and is targeted at civil and political activists with a view to enhance informed action. The Academia is part of Al Sharq Forum, an independent international non-profit organization that aims at consolidating the values of pluralism and justice. View Profile View Video
Our Mission

Al Sharq Academia shares the mission of its umbrella organization, Al Sharq Forum, that aims at consolidating the values of pluralism and justice while developing long-term strategies and programs that contribute to the political development, economic prosperity, and social cohesion for the people of the Al Sharq region and the world.
